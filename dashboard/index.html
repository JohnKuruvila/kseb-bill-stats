KSEB Solar Dashboard

Explore your KSEB bill history by switching between a quick overview, energy behavior, cost breakdowns, and the full monthly ledger. The dashboard treats each bill as the usage for the previous month, while the underlying data keeps the original bill date from your downloaded archive.

Cost reading guide
Use this tab to compare the bill total against its fixed and variable components. The trend tab in Overview shows how those charges move against demand for each usage month.
Fixed base
Fixed charge, duty, and meter rent explain the monthly floor even when net billed units are low.
Variable layer
Energy charge grows with billed usage, which is why a strong solar month reduces the most expensive part of the bill.
