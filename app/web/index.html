KSEB bills & Kerala electricity cost

Understand KSEB electricity bill charges and cost per unit

The dashboard turns your KSEB bills into month-by-month insights: KSEB import vs solar, bill amount trends, charge composition (fixed + energy charges, electricity duty, and meter rent), and cost per home unit (kWh). Use it to compare cycles and see how solar self-consumption changes your Kerala electricity cost.

How is electricity cost per unit calculated?

It divides each billing cycle's total amount by the derived home demand (kWh), separating solar self-used from solar exported to the grid.

What charge components are included in KSEB bill analysis?

Fixed charges, energy charges, electricity duty, meter rent, and remaining adjustments are summarized in the cost breakdown view.

Why do graphs show month-on-month usage trends?

Billing records often map to the previous month's usage. Charts normalize usage month while keeping the original bill date for reference.
